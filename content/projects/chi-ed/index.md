Name: Chi_ed 
Chi-Ed is an interactive dashboard and automated reporting tool that analyzes public high school outcomes and infrastructure across Chicago. The platform allows users to explore neighborhood-level disparities in key metrics and also enables direct comparisons between public schools. To goal is to facilitate an analysis of education gaps and to help students and families make informed high school choices.
